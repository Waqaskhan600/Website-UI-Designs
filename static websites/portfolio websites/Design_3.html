About Me

Biotechnology and Genetic Engineering professional with expertise in cancer genetics research

Professional Summary

I am a dedicated Biotechnology and Genetic Engineering researcher currently pursuing my M.Phil. at The University of Agriculture Peshawar. My research focuses on cancer genetics, specifically investigating the impact of deleterious EGFR polymorphisms in breast cancer through computational and experimental approaches.

With hands-on experience in molecular techniques, bioinformatics analysis, and research writing, I aim to contribute to the understanding of genetic mechanisms in disease development and progression.

My expertise spans DNA extraction, PCR, gel electrophoresis, genotyping, SNP analysis, and the use of various bioinformatics tools and databases. I'm also proficient in programming with Python and R for data analysis and machine learning applications in healthcare.
